Exercises

Here is a list of semester-long courses that I have taught or TAed in the past. Being a teaching assistant at EPFL is comprised of answering questions of the students during the exercises and grading the homeworks and exams. I was always a “principal TA”, which means administrating the course webpage (or the Moodle page and forums) and preparation of the exams in addition. At the other universities, my work additionally consisted of independent in-classroom teaching of the regular exercise classes. I consider myself adept in teaching various regression methods and time series analysis, as well as introductory probability and statistical inference classes for students of various backgrounds, inclcuding mathematicians, computer scientists, economists and engineers.
